Edificio CCB / CCB Building

The CCB Building is a hybrid civic and institutional space located in the heart of Cuenca, Ecuador. Designed to mediate between heritage and contemporary public use, the project reinterprets the colonial courtyard typology to host exhibitions, meetings, and learning activities in a spatially porous and inclusive environment.
